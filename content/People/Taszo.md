Shin monk sent to find [[Talenat]] and his [[Honorblade]] was originally journeying with a contingent of fellow monks but everyone except him were assassinated by [[Glyph Bandits]]. He died after entrusting his holy mission to the party while sending them to the assassin [[Liss]]. [[Po'ahu]] seems to have been bonding with him before his death.
